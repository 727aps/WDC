Women's Development Cell

Event Series 2026

The Women's Development Cell (WDC) is committed to fostering a safe, supportive, and empowering environment for women on campus. This curated event series for 2026 brings together workshops, discussions, health initiatives, and engaging activities designed to build confidence, promote well-being, and encourage inclusive participation from students and faculty members.
