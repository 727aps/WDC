Women in Development Cell

This Women’s Day, the Women in Development Cell of PSG College of Technology presents a week-long celebration dedicated to empowerment, well-being, and community building. As part of the Women’s Day celebrations, WDC is hosting a curated series of events throughout the week, bringing together workshops, discussions, health initiatives, and engaging activities designed to inspire and uplift.
